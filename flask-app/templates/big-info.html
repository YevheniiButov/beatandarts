BIG Registration Procedure

1. Credential Evaluation (IDW)

Submit your foreign diploma and qualifications for recognition through IDW (Internationale Diplomawaardering).

2. Language Proficiency

Demonstrate proficiency in Dutch (minimum B2 level), including medical Dutch, through official exams.

3. BI-Toets (Basic Medical Knowledge)

Pass the BI exam, which tests your basic medical knowledge and understanding of the Dutch healthcare system.

4. Professional Practice Evaluation

Complete clinical internships or assessments depending on your experience and the outcome of your credential evaluation.

5. Apply for BIG Registration

After completing all requirements, submit your application for inclusion in the BIG register and receive your official license to work as a dentist in the Netherlands.
